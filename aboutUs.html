Services & Facilities

A well-liked option for a taxi service in Colombo, Sri Lanka, CAR RENT4U provides a wide range of transportation services on a chauffeur-driven or self-drive basis.
We provide a seamless and uninterrupted service that is tailored to your needs, whether it be for corporate transportation, a tour of Sri Lanka, a wedding, or any other special occasion.

SELF DRIVE RENTAL

There are many different self-driving cars available in Sri Lanka, ranging from affordable to luxurious on daily, weekly, and monthly terms. GPS/SAT navigation units, baby / child seats, road maps and other optional equipment are available on request.

TOURS / CHAUFFEUR DRIVEN

At CAR RENT4U providing you with the best experience in Sri Lanka is our highest priority. we understand your arrangements for travel would have to be more than merely a means to a destination; in fact a crucial element in your experience of this glorious island country.

AIRPORT / CITY TRANSFERS

These airport transfers, city transfers and hotel transfers are available with a best rate guarantee for a certified service that ensures Euro NCAP certified vehicles with safety features including belts for all passengers, comprehensive ‘Rent A Car’ Insurance Cover.

WEDDINGS AND SPECIAL EVENTS

We are prepared for weddings and special occasions with a fleet of luxurious wedding cars, ensuring that the day's transportation goes without a hitch and on time. Our extensive fleet ensures that there is always a backup in case of a sudden emergency.

OPTIONAL EQUIPMENT

Those who are looking for a rental car service in Sri Lanka can choose from a wide selection of optional equipment that we offer. Such as GPS/SAT NAV Units, Child/Baby seats in different sizes, Maps and Guides, Cool Boxes and Mobile Phones.

ROAD SIDE ASSISTANCE

We offer 24/7/365 roadside assistance islandwide with a team ready for action. A pool of replacement vehicles is held at all times to ensure peace of mind. We are supported by a network of garages spread out across the island as well as immediate insurance assistance.
